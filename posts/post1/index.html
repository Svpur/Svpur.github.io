Sun Yat-sen University: A Beacon of Education and Innovation

Founded in 1924 by Dr. Sun Yat-sen, a great national hero, a great patriot and a great pioneer of China's democratic revolution, and jointly founded by the early leaders of the Communist Party of China, Sun Yat-sen University is one of the important cradling points for the spread of Marxism in China, with a fine revolutionary tradition, bright red genes and outstanding character pursuit. Sun Yat-sen University was originally called National Guangdong University. After the death of Dr. Sun Yat-sen, the university was renamed National Sun Yat-sen University in 1926.

Today's Sun Yat-sen University was formed by the merger of Sun Yat-sen University and Sun Yat-sen University of Medical Sciences in October 2001. Through the joint construction of the ministry and the province, and with the strong support of the state, the local and the society, Sun Yat-sen University has become a first-class and internationally renowned modern comprehensive university, forming a comprehensive, research-oriented and open school-running characteristics.

In the history of running a school for nearly a hundred years, Sun Yat-sen University has gathered a large number of famous masters at home and abroad, and has a profound academic background. Deng Zhiyi, Ding Ying, Feng Youlan, Guo Moruo, Lu Xun, Fu Snian, Gu Jiegang, Shang Chengzuo, He Sijing, Zhao Yuanren, Zhou Gucheng, Liang Boqiang, Pu Zelong, Wang Ya Nan, Liu Jie, Cen Zhongmian, Wang Qi, Ke Lin, Chen Yin-Ke, Jiang Lifu, Yang Rongguo, Liang Fangzhong, Rong Geng, Gao Zhao-lan, Xie Zhiguang, Chen Yaozhen, Chen Xintao, Lin Shumo, Qin Guangyu, Zhong Shifan, Zhou Shoukai, Dong Fen Kan, Dai Ling, Gao Youxi and other famous scholars have taught in the school. In recent years, Sun Yat-sen University has attracted a large number of outstanding academic talents from home and abroad, striving to be in the front line of teaching and research, and is committed to training more and more excellent builders and successors of the socialist cause.

In September 1999, Zhuhai Municipal People's Government and Sun Yat-sen University officially signed an agreement on the construction of Sun Yat-sen University Zhuhai Campus. In January 2000, the Ministry of Education approved the construction of Zhongshan University Zhuhai campus. In September 2015, Shenzhen Municipal People's Government and Sun Yat-sen University signed a memorandum of understanding on the cooperation of Sun Yat-sen University Shenzhen Campus; In December of the same year, the Ministry of Education approved the construction of Shenzhen campus. At this point, Sun Yat-sen University has formed a school-running pattern of three campuses and five campuses, taking root in Guangzhou, Zhuhai and Shenzhen, with the overall planning and dislocation development of each campus, and the three campuses and five campuses jointly supporting the high-quality and concursive development of Sun Yat-sen University.

In recent years, on the basis of strengthening the traditional advantaged disciplines of arts, science and medicine, Sun Yat-sen University has made efforts to fill in the shortcomings of engineering, fill the gaps in agriculture and art, and become more complete in the disciplines, forming a discipline pattern for the comprehensive development of arts, science, medicine, industry and agriculture. Guangzhou campus focuses on improving the traditional advantages of liberal arts, science and medicine; Zhuhai campus focuses on the development of deep sea, deep space, deep Earth and deep blue subject groups; Shenzhen campus focuses on the development of new medicine, new engineering and new agricultural subjects. The comprehensive advantages and characteristics of the school are increasingly prominent, and the discipline strength ranks among the forefront of domestic universities.

The discipline and scientific research plan focuses on major national strategies, serves the needs of regional social and economic development, seize the important opportunities in the development of the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area, further promote organized scientific research, promote interdisciplinary and integrated development, stimulate new momentum for the development of humanities and social sciences, and make a major contribution to the construction of China's independent knowledge system.

The medical department of Sun Yat-sen University has a long history and excellent strength, and the earliest Western medicine education in China was born here. At present, the medical education and teaching reform of Sun Yat-sen University has been deeply promoted, and the innovation of medical research is changing with each passing day. It has 10 directly affiliated hospitals, and has built a medical treatment service network system with complete categories, strong strength, leading technology, advanced equipment, complementary advantages and strong sense of social responsibility. The comprehensive strength of medical education and research, medical service ability and scale rank among the leading ranks in China.
